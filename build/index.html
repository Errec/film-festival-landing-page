The Festival

Come spend a night under the stars Saturday through Monday, August 5th to 8th, 2018 at the 21st Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival. This FREE event showcases the recent hit and classic favorite feature movies celebrating the end of summer and benefitting NIH Children’s Charities. The Festival, featuring the only nine-story high, 52-foot wide inflatable movie screen within 1,000 miles, offers an unrivaled outdoor movie experience with digital cinema projection.

WEATHER: In case of inclement weather, outdoor movies are cancelled. Visit www.brooklyn.news12.com/weather or follow BOFF on Facebook and Twitter for up-to-date information.
